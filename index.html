becoming posthuman

call for art

becoming posthuman is a one-day multimedia event in boston, massachusetts focusing on digital culture. The event will be kickstarted in the afternoon by a series of short talks and workshops, and followed by a night of visual art, performances, and music.

our idea of posthumanism is not anti-humanism, but rather a proposal to examine and question the historical notions of “human nature”, and challenge typical notions of human subjectivity and embodiment.

we are calling for submissions of visual art of all mediums, proposal for talks and/or workshops, and anything (including activities) you could see fit into this event.

we are looking for works that address topics including but not limited to: digital intimacy, online identity, computer graphics, internet of things, robotics, artificial intelligence, virtual reality, cyber-poetry, crypto-currency, cyberfeminism, cybernetics, open-source culture, cyberwarfare, decentralization and the internet, sex and the internet, etc.

for submissions of artwork, include a link to portfolio website, instagram, or Youtube/Vimeo, a short artist bio (less than 100 words), a short artist statement (less than 100 words), and pictures or links to videos of work being submitted.

for submissions of talks and workshops, include a short bio and a proposal.

all submissions should be emailed to becomingposthuman@gmail.com

deadline extended to: 10/15/2017
